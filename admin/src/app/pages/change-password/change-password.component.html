Safety Best Practices

  • Never share your admin password with anyone.
  • Avoid reusing passwords from personal accounts (e.g. social media, personal mail).
  • Ensure your passwords contain upper/lower case letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • We recommend using a trusted password manager to generate and store high-entropy keys.
